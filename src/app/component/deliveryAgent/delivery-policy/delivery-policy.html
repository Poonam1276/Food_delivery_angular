Delivery Agent Policy

Payment Policy

Delivery agents are paid in cash for each completed delivery. Please ensure payment is collected from the customer at the time of delivery. Any discrepancies should be reported immediately.

Delivery Guidelines

  • Verify the order before leaving the restaurant.
  • Ensure timely and safe delivery.
  • Update order status in the app promptly.
  • Maintain hygiene and professionalism.

Code of Conduct

  • Be respectful and courteous to customers.
  • Follow traffic rules and safety protocols.
  • Do not tamper with food packages.
